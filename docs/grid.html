Alignment

Vertical Align

You can easily vertically center things by adding the class .top-vertical, .mid-vertical and .bottom-vertical to the container holding the items you want to vertically align.

<div class="top-vertical">Centered top vertically</div>
<div class="mid-vertical">Centered mid vertically</div>
<div class="bottom-vertical">Centered bottom vertically</div>

Text Align

These classes are for horizontally aligning content: .left-align, .right-align and .center-align.

<div class="left-align">Left Align</div>
<div class="center-align">Center Align</div>
<div class="right-align">Right Align</div>

Quick Floats

Quickly float things by adding the class left or right to the element.

<div class="left">Left Float</div>
<div class="right">Right Float</div>
